## 🚀 Key Capabilities

### 1. 🤖 "Cybernetic" Coaching (Zero-Apology Protocol)
FITNESSAI is a highly disciplined coach. It uses a **Zero-Apology Protocol**: if you point out an error or update a constraint (e.g., "I told you my knee hurts"), it will not waste time with conversational fluff or apologies. It instantly confirms the error and outputs the corrected, safe workout table. Every response is anchored with `[FITNESSAI: ACTIVE]` to ensure the AI never breaks character during long coaching sessions.

### 2. 🧠 Mandatory Pre-Computation (Analysis)
Before generating any workout table, FITNESSAI outputs a brief `**Analysis:**` section. It explains *why* it chose a specific periodization, volume strategy, or exercise substitution based on your exact constraints, ensuring you understand the logic behind the program.

### 3. 🔬 Modern Hypertrophy Science
FITNESSAI programs reflect the latest peer-reviewed sports science:
* **Lengthened Partial Repetitions:** Isolation exercises emphasize the stretched portion of the range of motion.
* **Effective Reps:** Programs count "stimulating reps" (those within ~3 reps of failure).
* **RIR + RPE Dual System:** Uses both Reps In Reserve and Rate of Perceived Exertion for precise intensity control.
* **Evidence-Based Rest Intervals:** 2–3 minutes for compound lifts, 60–90 seconds for isolation work.
* **Mandatory Eccentric Control:** Every rep includes a controlled lowering phase (minimum 2 seconds).

### 4. 📋 Anti-Lazy Protocol (Complete Plans)
A critical safeguard ensuring you receive **gym-ready programs**, not shortcuts:
* **Every training day is fully written out** — no "Repeat Day 1" or "Same as above."
* **All prescriptions are exact** — "3 sets," not "3–4 sets." Specific to your level.
* **Long plans are segmented** — multi-week programs split across messages with a `<continue>` prompt, never shortened or collapsed.
* **Result:** You can screenshot any day and take it directly to the gym.

### 5. 💊 Evidence-Based Supplement Guidance
When asked, FITNESSAI provides an honest, research-backed supplement assessment:
* **Tier A (Strong Evidence):** Creatine Monohydrate, Caffeine, Protein Powder, Vitamin D3.
* **Tier B (Situational):** Magnesium, Omega-3.
* **Tier F (Waste of Money):** BCAAs, Fat Burners, Test Boosters, Detox products — clearly labeled with explanations of *why* they don't work.

### 6. 🌍 Universal Language Support
* **Input:** Speak naturally in **ANY language** (Ukrainian, Spanish, German, etc.).
* **Output:** The system responds ENTIRELY in your native language, translating all headers, tables, and cues.
* **Terminology:** Universal terms (RPE, RIR, HIIT) are kept as acronyms but explained in your local language on first use. Exercise names are localized or transliterated.

## 🛡️ Safety & Edge Cases

FITNESSAI includes comprehensive, non-negotiable safety protocols:

* **Immediate Stop Triggers:** Chest pain, dizziness, severe joint pain, recent surgery, pregnancy, heart conditions, uncontrolled hypertension → all halt programming and refer to a physician.
* **Age < 16:** Conservative bodyweight programming with parental guidance recommended.
* **Age 50+:** Extended warm-ups, RPE ceiling of 7/10, balance work, extra rest.
* **BMI 35+:** Seated/supported exercises, gradual progression, joint-stress monitoring.
* **Unrealistic Expectations:** Educates on sustainable rates and reframes goals.
* **Eating Disorder Red Flags:** Recognized and redirected to qualified professionals.
* **PED/Steroid Inquiries:** Declined; redirected to sports medicine.
* **No Equipment + Physical Limitations:** Creative solutions (chair exercises, wall movements, isometrics).
