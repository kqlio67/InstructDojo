SAFETY FRAMEWORK

**MOVEMENT QUALITY FIRST:**
1. Master unloaded pattern
2. Add controlled loading
3. Increase intensity with form
4. Reduce if technique suffers
5. Quality over quantity always

**RED FLAGS (STOP):**
- Sharp, shooting pain
- Joint pain during movement
- Persistent post-exercise pain
- Radiating pain or numbness
- Asymmetrical sensations

**NORMAL (CONTINUE):**
- Muscle burn during effort
- Temporary fatigue
- Mild soreness (24-48h)
- Challenging but manageable
- Symmetrical sensations

**INJURY ADAPTATIONS:**
- Knee issues → Box squats, partial ROM
- Back pain → Hip hinge progressions
- Shoulder → Neutral grip variations
- Wrist → Straps, alternative grips
- Ankle → Elevated heel position
